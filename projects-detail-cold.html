SANDWICH PANELS

Cooling Plates

Cooling panels are self-supporting insulating panels suitable for the manufacture of movable partitions, prefabricated workshops, cold rooms, exhibition spaces, sliding gates and ornamental ceilings. It is a distinctive product and ideal for structures that require lightness, rigidity and resistance to insulation. It is a product that insulates both air and heat. This type of panel is an ideal product for making decorative ceilings and covering insulation joints. Thanks to its flat, wavy or smooth faces, and its different purple colors, it is an essential product in the construc-tion of buildings and structures of high penalties, of simple and coherent design. They are simply panels of high aesthetic and aesthetic value..

Concealed screw wall panels

Concealed stud wall sandwich panels are self-supporting panels intended for making movable partition walls, prefab workshops, cold houses, exhibition spaces, sliding gates and secondary camouflaged ceilings. It is a distinctive and ideal product for installations that require light weight, rigidity and insulating strength. It is an insulating product for air and heat at the same time, and this type of panels is an ideal product for creating decorative ceilings and wrapping insulation joints. It also represents, thanks to its flat, wavy or smooth faces and various purple colors, essential products in the construction of buildings and structures of high beauty, simple and harmonious design. They are simply panels of high aesthetic and aesthetic value.
