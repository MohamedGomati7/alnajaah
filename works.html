OUR WORKS

Specification of sandwich panel :

Sandwich panels offer good solutions for cladding, thermal insulation, water and air leakage, structural fire safety and environmental protection.
It also provides economical, fast and efficient solutions according to customer requirements. The insulation boards consist of two integrated layers of sheet steel and are filled with polyurethane between them.
Polyurethane has very high thermal insulation properties, light weight and fire resistance.
The steel sheet layers are hot-dip galvanized and coated with a layer of polyester and steel sheet for the insulation boards which are electrically painted with a durable material with dust-proof properties on both sides.


    The sandwich panels are the best option to establish because they are :

  • Lightweight.
  • Practical and quick to install.
  • Insulated from heat and mois-ture.
  • Soundproof.
  • Easy to sterilize.
  • Easy to clean.
  • Environmentally friendly and contributes to reducing the spread of fires.
  • Sustainable.
